# Slide Content Guidelines

## Simplification Rules

1. **One idea per slide**: Each slide should focus on a single concept
2. **Bullet points**: Convert paragraphs to concise bullet points (3-5 per slide)
3. **Short text**: Keep bullet points under 10 words when possible
4. **Remove filler**: Eliminate unnecessary words and phrases
5. **Use headings**: Create clear, descriptive slide titles

## Content Structure

- **Title slide**: Title + subtitle/author
- **Agenda slide**: List main topics (optional)
- **Content slides**: Heading + key points
- **Summary slide**: Key takeaways

## Text Guidelines

- Avoid complete sentences; use phrases
- Use active voice
- Highlight keywords with **bold**
- Use consistent formatting throughout
